Important Notes

Booking Terms & Information

Confirmation Timeline

All reservation requests are reviewed and confirmed by our dispatch team within 30 minutes during business hours. Same-day requests are handled as a priority — for urgent bookings, call 1 (800) 475-9975 directly for an immediate response.

Within 30 minutes Email confirmation

Flat-Rate Pricing

Your confirmed fare is a guaranteed flat rate. There are no surge charges, no meter fares and no hidden fees. Tolls are disclosed in your confirmation. View our Fare page for route-based pricing.

Airport Pickups

Provide your flight number so we can track your arrival in real time. Complimentary wait time of 60 minutes applies from your actual landing time. Your chauffeur will be waiting with a name sign at arrivals.

Cancellations & Changes

Cancellations with 24+ hours notice incur no charge. Changes can be made at any time — contact dispatch by phone or WhatsApp. Read our full Delays & Cancellations Policy.

Vehicle Availability

Vehicle assignment is subject to availability at the time of confirmation. If your preferred vehicle is unavailable, we will suggest the closest alternative and confirm before finalising your booking.

Advance Booking

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance for guaranteed vehicle selection. For large groups, weddings and peak holiday periods, 48–72 hours notice ensures the best availability.

Group & Corporate

Multi-vehicle group bookings are handled by our operations team. Corporate account clients benefit from consolidated invoicing and a dedicated account manager. Contact us to arrange.
