Our Policy

Delays & Cancellations

We understand that travel plans change. Our policy is built around flexibility, transparency and respect for your time — while ensuring our chauffeurs and operations are fairly protected. Please read this page carefully before booking.

Read the Policy Contact Support

Policy Effective Date

This Delays & Cancellations policy was last reviewed and updated in January 2026. It applies to all bookings made with Book N Ride Executive Car Service. By completing a reservation, you confirm that you have read and agreed to these terms.

Updated January 2026 All bookings CT · NY · NJ

Flight Delays

Book N Ride monitors all airport pickup flights in real time using live flight tracking data. If your inbound flight is delayed, your chauffeur's arrival time is automatically adjusted — you do not need to call us. Our dispatch team is already aware of the situation and managing it proactively.

The following applies to all flight-tracked airport pickups:

  • Complimentary wait time begins from the updated actual landing time, not the originally scheduled arrival time.
  • If your flight is diverted to an alternate airport, please notify us as soon as possible so we can reposition your chauffeur accordingly.
  • If your flight is cancelled, contact our dispatch immediately at 1 (800) 475-9975 to reschedule your pickup for the next available flight.
  • We do not charge additional fees for standard flight delays that are monitored and adjusted through our tracking system.

Traffic & Road Delays

Book N Ride chauffeurs use real-time traffic monitoring to select the fastest available route to your pickup location. In the event of significant traffic, accidents, road closures or severe weather, your chauffeur will depart early to absorb anticipated delays.

In rare cases where conditions are beyond our control and may impact your pickup time:

  • Your chauffeur or our dispatch team will contact you directly to advise of any expected delay.
  • We recommend allowing additional travel buffer time for airport pickups during peak periods, major events and severe weather.
  • Book N Ride accepts no liability for delays caused by events outside our reasonable control, including but not limited to accidents, extreme weather, road closures or third-party incidents.

Complimentary Wait Time

We understand that travel involves unpredictable moments. Book N Ride includes a complimentary wait time period with every booking to accommodate normal variations in arrival and readiness.

  • Airport pickups: 60 minutes of complimentary wait time from the updated actual landing time.
  • All other pickups (residential, hotel, office): 15 minutes of complimentary wait time from the scheduled pickup time.
  • Cruise terminal pickups: 30 minutes of complimentary wait time from the scheduled disembarkation time.

After the complimentary wait period has elapsed, an additional waiting charge will apply at the prevailing hourly rate, billed in 15-minute increments. Your chauffeur will remain on location for a reasonable time. If contact cannot be made after the extended wait period, the booking may be treated as a no-show (see below).

Important

If you anticipate being significantly late for your pickup, please call or message our dispatch team as early as possible. We will hold your chauffeur and adjust the booking at no additional charge whenever we can.

Cancellation Policy

We ask that all cancellations be made by contacting our dispatch team directly — by phone at 1 (800) 475-9975, by WhatsApp or by email. Cancellations must be confirmed by a Book N Ride team member to be considered valid.

  • Cancellation with 24+ hours notice: No charge. Full cancellation at no penalty.
  • Cancellation with 12–24 hours notice: A cancellation fee of 25% of the total booking value applies.
  • Cancellation with 4–12 hours notice: A cancellation fee of 50% of the total booking value applies.
  • Cancellation with less than 4 hours notice: A cancellation fee of 75% of the total booking value applies.
  • No-show (chauffeur on location, client unreachable): The full booking amount is charged.

Cancellation fees reflect the real costs incurred when a chauffeur has been dispatched, a vehicle prepared and time blocked for your journey. We appreciate your understanding.

No-Show Policy

A booking is considered a no-show when:

  • Your chauffeur arrives at the designated pickup location at the confirmed time.
  • Our dispatch team is unable to reach you by phone or message within the complimentary wait period.
  • No alternative arrangement has been agreed with our team prior to the pickup time.

In the event of a no-show, the full booking amount will be charged and no refund will be issued. If you believe a no-show charge has been applied in error, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of the scheduled pickup and we will review the case promptly.

Booking Modifications

We understand that plans change. We will always do our best to accommodate modifications to existing bookings, subject to vehicle availability.

  • Date or time changes: Contact us as early as possible. Changes made with more than 24 hours notice are accommodated at no extra charge.
  • Vehicle upgrades: Upgrades may be requested at any time and are subject to availability. Additional fare charges will apply.
  • Route changes: Pickup or drop-off location changes made before dispatch are accommodated where possible. If the route change results in a materially different journey, a revised quote will be provided.
  • Passenger count changes: Additions that exceed the capacity of the booked vehicle may require a larger vehicle, subject to availability and revised pricing.

To modify your booking, contact our dispatch team at 1 (800) 475-9975 or via WhatsApp. Modifications are only confirmed when acknowledged by a Book N Ride team member.

Refunds

Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to the original payment method. The timeline may vary depending on your bank or card issuer.

  • Refunds are issued only for cancellations that qualify under the policy above.
  • Partial refunds will reflect the cancellation fee applicable at the time of cancellation.
  • Refund requests must be submitted to our team within 30 days of the original booking date.
  • Disputes outside this window are reviewed on a case-by-case basis at our discretion.

Exceptional Circumstances & Force Majeure

In the event of circumstances beyond either party's reasonable control — including but not limited to severe weather emergencies, declared states of emergency, major transport infrastructure failures, civil unrest or natural disasters — Book N Ride reserves the right to cancel or reschedule affected bookings without penalty to either party.

In such cases, we will:

  • Notify you as early as possible by phone or message.
  • Offer an alternative booking date at the same rate, or
  • Issue a full refund if no suitable alternative can be arranged.

We will always communicate transparently and work to find the most reasonable resolution for every affected client.

Group & Corporate Bookings

Group bookings involving three or more vehicles, and all corporate account bookings, are subject to bespoke cancellation terms agreed at the time of reservation. Please refer to your booking confirmation or corporate account agreement for the specific terms applicable to your booking.

If you are unsure of the cancellation terms applicable to your group or corporate booking, contact our team for clarification before cancelling.
