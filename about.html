Our Story

From One Vehicle to Connecticut's Premier Fleet

Book N Ride Executive Car Service was founded in 1997 with a single mission: to provide the kind of luxury transportation that makes every passenger feel like the most important person in the room. What began as a single vehicle serving Fairfield County has grown into a full-fleet operation covering Connecticut, New York and New Jersey.

We are a family-owned company built on values — reliability, discretion and an obsessive attention to detail. Every vehicle in our fleet is late-model, fully inspected and hand-detailed before every single trip. Every chauffeur is licensed, background-checked and trained to the highest professional standards.

Today, Book N Ride serves executives, families, wedding parties, corporations and sports teams across the tri-state area and beyond. Our commitment remains exactly what it was on day one: get you there on time, in comfort and in style.
