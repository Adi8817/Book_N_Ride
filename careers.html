What We Offer

Employee Benefits

We believe that how we treat our team is a direct reflection of how our team treats our clients. These are not perks — they are our commitment to every person who works with us.

Competitive Base Pay

Above-market rates for all roles, reviewed annually and benchmarked against industry standards.

Insurance Coverage

Comprehensive commercial insurance covers all chauffeurs while on duty, including liability and accident coverage.

Gratuity & Tips

All gratuities received on trips go directly to the chauffeur — we never withhold or redirect driver tips.

Certification Support

We support team members pursuing chauffeur certifications, TLC licensing and defensive driving credentials.

Paid Onboarding

All new hires are paid for their onboarding period including vehicle familiarisation, systems training and safety briefings.

Flexible Hours

Shift preferences are respected wherever operationally possible. We work to build schedules that work for your life.

Late-Model Vehicles

You will never drive an outdated or poorly maintained vehicle. Our fleet is refreshed regularly and kept immaculate.

Career Growth

Long-term team members move into senior chauffeur, lead dispatcher and fleet management roles as the company grows.
