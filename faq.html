03

Airport Transfers

We use live flight tracking data to monitor every inbound flight linked to an airport pickup. If your flight is delayed, diverted or arrives early, our dispatch team is automatically notified and your chauffeur's schedule is adjusted accordingly. You do not need to call us — we are already managing it.
For airport pickups, we include 60 minutes of complimentary wait time from the updated actual landing time. For residential, hotel and office pickups, we include 15 minutes from the scheduled time. For cruise terminal pickups, we include 30 minutes from the scheduled disembarkation time. After the complimentary period, a waiting fee applies in 15-minute increments.
Please contact our dispatch team as soon as you learn of the cancellation. Call us at 1 (800) 475-9975 or send a WhatsApp message. We will reschedule your pickup for your next confirmed flight at no additional charge whenever possible. Please see our Delays & Cancellations page for the full policy.
Yes. Our chauffeurs meet arriving passengers inside the terminal holding a personalised name sign. They will assist with luggage, guide you to the vehicle and ensure a smooth transition from the arrivals hall to your destination. Meet-and-greet is available at all major airports we serve.
We serve all major airports in the tri-state area including JFK International, LaGuardia (LGA), Newark Liberty (EWR), Westchester County (HPN), Bradley International (BDL), Tweed-New Haven (HVN), Teterboro (TEB) and Long Island MacArthur (ISP). We also serve private airfields on request.
04

Pricing & Payment

Book N Ride operates on a flat-rate pricing model based on your route, vehicle type and trip requirements. Your fare is calculated and quoted at the time of booking — there is no metered billing, no surge pricing and no hidden fees. Tolls are disclosed upfront and included in your quote where applicable.
Your quoted flat rate covers your journey as booked. Additional charges may apply for: extended wait time beyond the complimentary period, additional stops not included in the original booking, vehicle upgrades or route changes requested after dispatch, and gratuity (discretionary — not automatically added). All potential extras are communicated transparently before they are applied.
We accept all major credit cards and debit cards. Corporate account clients may arrange invoiced billing on net-30 or net-60 terms by agreement. For specific payment queries, please contact our team at the time of booking or when setting up a corporate account.
The fastest way to get a quote is to call our dispatch at 1 (800) 475-9975 or send us a WhatsApp message with your pickup location, destination, date, time and number of passengers. You can also use our online reservation system for an instant quote. All quotes are firm, flat-rate and obligation-free.
05

Cancellations & Changes

Cancellations made 24 or more hours in advance incur no charge. Short-notice cancellations are subject to a tiered fee: 25% for 12–24 hours, 50% for 4–12 hours, and 75% for less than 4 hours. No-shows are charged in full. For the complete policy, visit our Delays & Cancellations page.
Yes. Contact our dispatch team at any time to request a date change, time change, vehicle upgrade or route adjustment. Modifications made with more than 24 hours notice are accommodated at no extra charge where operationally possible. Changes made on shorter notice are handled on a best-effort basis.
Contact us immediately if you anticipate being late — call or WhatsApp our dispatch. We will do our best to hold your chauffeur and adjust the pickup at no additional charge. After the complimentary wait period has elapsed, a waiting fee applies in 15-minute increments. Early communication is always the best approach.
The complete policy — covering wait times, cancellation tiers, no-show terms, modification procedures, refund timelines, force majeure and group booking terms — is available on our Delays & Cancellations page.
