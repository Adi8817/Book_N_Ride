General Questions

For a quick booking of the car service, you better make a reservation is through our “Online Quotes & Reservations” page on our website or simply call our customer support. Once we receive your reservation, we will check all the information and send you a confirmation.
Your driver will meet you in an exact destination where you are and they will confirm everything in advance. Also, you will receive exact instructions of the meeting point in your email confirmation when you make the booking, as well as the driver, will call you to know where you are and from where to pick up. For airport pickup, we ensure using your flight number to track your flight so that your driver is waiting for you when you land. We also monitor delays and update the time we send your driver in accordingly. We can wait 15-30 minutes for you and after this waiting time fees are charged accordingly.
We offer a wide variety of vehicles designed to accommodate the needs of all, whether you are a single passenger, small groups and larger passenger groups. In order to know what kind of the fleet we have, you simply click on the fleet tab to review our fleet. With years of experience in the business, we understand that a successful event depends on the successful arrival and departure of the passengers and to make it possible we have purchased late model fleets of all shapes and sizes, including- SUVs to sprinter ford 10, Sprinter Benz 8 passengers, Lincoln stretch 12 passengers and various others.
We do have a cancellation policy, which has made to help you to give great benefits and peace of mind. In order to cancel a reservation, please call on xx-xxx-xxx. Cancellations must be received at least two hours in advance of the service pickup time for pickups. And the reservations for service elsewhere may require 4 to 24 hours cancellation, especially for sedan and SUV service days for buses.
