Fare Notes & Disclaimer

  • All fares shown are one-way, base fares and are subject to confirmation at the time of booking. Prices may vary based on exact pickup address, stops en route, vehicle availability and seasonal demand.
  • Applicable tolls are not included in the base fare and will be disclosed upfront when your quote is confirmed.
  • Gratuity is at the client's discretion and is not automatically added to any fare.
  • Fares are valid for standard one-way trips with one pickup address and one drop-off. Additional stops are quoted separately.
  • Return or round-trip bookings qualify for our round-trip rate — ask at the time of booking.
  • Airport wait time: 60 minutes of complimentary wait time is included from the updated actual landing time. After this period, a waiting charge applies.
  • Fares for routes not listed — including New Jersey origins, hotel-to-airport, cruise terminals and interstate routes — are available on request. Call 1 (800) 475-9975 or WhatsApp for an instant custom quote.
  • Book N Ride reserves the right to update pricing without prior notice. The confirmed fare in your booking email is the fare you will be charged.
